How to Make Bulgogi

Description

If you've been craving a taste of Korea, beef bulgogi is sure to satisfy it and some. Dating back to the Goguryeo era, bulgogi is best paired with rice and will have you craving seconds, thirds, and fourths!

Beef Bulgogi is relativly easy to make but does require some experience cooking with marinated meats. In this recipe, you'll learn how to make beef bulgogi and the ingredients needed.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbl soy sauce
  • 0.25 cup chopped green onion
  • 2.25 tbl white sugar
  • 2 tbl minced garlic
  • 2 tbl sesame seeds
  • 2 tbl sesame oil
  • 0.25 ground black pepper
  • 1 lbs flank steak, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.
  3. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
