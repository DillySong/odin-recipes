How to Make A Vanilla Milkshake

  • Home
  • Original Recipe

Description

There's no better way then combining the principles of a dessert and the medium of a drink then with a vanilla milk shake.

A vanilla milkshake is easy to make but hard to resist. In this recipe, you'll learn how to make a basic shake and the ingredients you'll need.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
